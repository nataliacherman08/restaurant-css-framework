The Gohan San

The Gohan San is a sushi restaurant located in the center of the beautiful Belgium's capital, Brussels.The restaurant has been at the service of taste and quality since its creation in 2012, where authenticity, hospitality and know-how have never ceased to combine over time.

The restaurant Gohan San offers authentic and modern Japanese cuisine, the chef revisits traditional dishes combining flavor, freshness, respect for the product and presentation. These are the assets to offer you a pleasant moment of taste.

Our air-conditioned room has a capacity of 50 places, for your banquets and receptions and for all other reservations.
